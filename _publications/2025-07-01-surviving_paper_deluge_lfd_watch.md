This Science and Technology Watch column presents a structured one-year review of the Learning from Demonstration (LfD) field, curating and synthesizing key contributions across imitation learning, policy learning, and robot programming by demonstration. The work identifies emerging trends, highlights representative results, and outlines open challenges for researchers and practitioners navigating this rapidly evolving area.
